By accessing and using our websites and/or the Zabibu Software, you agree to review this Privacy Policy, which may be changed or updated at any time without notice.

Any changes will be posted on the Site and you are advised to consult it regularly.

    We also collect and process data about our Users' preferences and traffic patterns on our sites (such as the IP addresses of visitors to our Site).

    • creating accounts on our websites (Zabibu Account, Trial Account, Account on our presentation platform) in order to access the Software and use our services;
    • the provision of our various websites (the Site, our training platform), and their various functionalities.
    • download our online resources;
    • creating accounts on our websites (Zabibu Account, Trial Account, Account on our presentation platform) in order to access the Software and use our services;
    • Management and follow-up of services (creation of services, creation of clients, payments, claims and support to users);
    • to improve our commercial service. In this context, we may record your telephone communications with our sales teams;
    • Customer satisfaction monitoring (surveys, customer opinion monitoring);
    • management of technological and commercial partnerships;
    • management of prospecting and information requests (sending commercial and marketing offers, newsletters, white papers, requests for online demonstrations, requests for quotes, etc.);
    • improving our services by placing cookies on users' terminals;

    To open an Account, you must provide us with at least the following personally identifiable information in order to use the Software:

    • Your name
    • Your first name
    • Your email address
    • Your phone number.
    • Your password

    You can complete your profile with other Personal Data (address, other phone number, date of birth, photo, names of your clients or prospects, etc.).

  2. 1.2.We collect information related to the contractual relationship we have with you: history, subscribed modules, billing and payment elements, requests and incidents reported to the support service...

  3. 1.3.We also collect data about you during telephone conversations, which may be recorded for the purpose of improving our service.

  4. 1.4.We collect, through (or via) certain cookies that are automatically placed on your terminal, or other cookies that are subject to your express consent, certain Personal Information when you visit our Site (zabibu.co) or when you use the Software: for example, the date, time and duration of your visit to our Site, the path of your navigation, the type of your operating system, etc.

    A cookie is a small text file sent by the website visited by the User to his or her Internet browser or to the device used. Its function is to allow the site to function, to facilitate navigation (memorize technical choices, past activity), to improve the use of the site and the service and to better understand the experience of the Users.

    The maximum lifetime of a cookie is 13 months.

    Our Cookie Policy is available here.

III. Processing of Personal Data

ZABIBU collects and processes your Personal Data fairly and lawfully, and in accordance with the principles of the European Regulation 2016/679 of 27 April 2016 (RGPD).

This Privacy Policy describes ZABIBU's obligations as a controller of your Personal Data under the GDPR.

ZABIBU has appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) who continuously monitors Zabibu's compliance with the principles and rules of the GDPR and can be contacted at dpo@zabibu.co.

The DPO’s mission includes: 

  • To inform and advise the Data Controller on the application of the RGPD in the company;
  • To make Zabibu employees aware of the protection of Users' Personal Data;
  • Support them when implementing treatments;
  • To respond to requests related to the exercise of your rights, in accordance with Article VIII.

IV. Retention of your Personal Data

4.1. Security

ZABIBU makes every effort to avoid the loss, misappropriation, intrusion, unauthorized disclosure, alteration or destruction of the Personal Data you provide to us.

Thus:

  • The Data is stored on our own servers, which are hosted within the AMAZON AWS infrastructure. The verification of the security of the servers and the updating of our Operating Software is carried out in real time.
  • All the information you send us is encrypted [TLS V1.3 protocol].
  • ZABIBU employees are subject to an obligation of confidentiality and non-disclosure and have all signed a specific commitment concerning the protection of Personal Data.
  • Access to your Data is governed by a strict access control policy, reserved for authorised persons, under defined conditions.
  • When we use service providers to process Personal Data, we have previously verified that these service providers guarantee an equivalent level of security protection.

For more information, see our Security FAQ, available here.

4.2. Duration

ZABIBU retains your Personal Data in accordance with the following legal and regulatory provisions :

  • We retain information relating to the management of the Customer account, orders, invoicing, payments 10 years after the end of the contract or the last contact from the inactive Customer.
  • We keep the information relating to the constitution and management of the prospecting files 3 years from the last contact from the prospect.
  • We retain inactive Customer Data for the purpose of sending information about our commercial and marketing offers, within 3 years of the end of the commercial relationship.
  • We keep records of telephone calls between you and our sales teams for a period of six (6) months.

  • We have the obligation to keep for 1 year the following Personal Data resulting from the creation, modification or deletion of User Content:

    • - Connection identification;
    • - The identification given by the terminal;
    • - Types of protocols;  
    • - Nature of the operation;
    • - Date and time of the operation;
    • - Identification used by the author of the operation.

When the retention of Data is no longer justified by the management of a Customer account, a legal obligation, commercial requirements, the establishment, exercise or defense of our legal rights, or when you request us to do so by application of one of your rights (Article VIII), we will securely delete your Data.

4.3. cancellation of account

You may also request that your Account be deleted in accordance with the T&Cs. Your Data will then be deleted in accordance with the article 4.2. above.

You have the ability to sync your Gmail account to the ZABIBUCRM Software via a login application. In order to enhance the security and privacy of your Gmail account user data, and notwithstanding any other provision of this Privacy Policy, the use of your data by the login application is subject to the following restrictions:

  • The login application uses access to your Gmail User Data only to perform the following actions: read, write, edit, or monitor Gmail message content (including attachments) and metadata, in order to provide a web-based email client that allows users to write, send, read, and process email;
  • The login application never transfers this Gmail data to third parties, unless necessary to provide and improve the functionality of the login application, to comply with applicable laws, or as part of a merger, acquisition or sale of assets;
  • The login application does not use this Gmail data to deliver advertisements;
  • The application does not allow a human being to read this data, unless you expressly authorize us to do so for specific emails, for security reasons (investigation of misuse), to comply with applicable regulations, or for internal intervention on the application provided that the data has been aggregated and anonymized before.
